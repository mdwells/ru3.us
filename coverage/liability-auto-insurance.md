# What Is Liability Auto Insurance Coverage?

**Liability auto insurance coverage is the part of an auto policy that pays for injuries and property damage you cause to others in an at-fault accident, up to the limits you select.**

## Key Facts

- Liability coverage is split into **bodily injury** and **property damage**.  
- It is required in almost every U.S. state.  
- It does NOT pay for your own vehicle or your own injuries.  
- Limits are expressed as numbers like **25/50/25**.

## Typical Liability Limits

| Format | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 25/50/25 | $25k per person, $50k per accident, $25k property damage |
| 50/100/50 | $50k per person, $100k per accident, $50k property damage |
