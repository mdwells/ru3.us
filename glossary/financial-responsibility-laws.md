# Financial Responsibility Laws

**Financial responsibility laws** require drivers to demonstrate that they can **pay for damages** they cause in an auto accident.

Most commonly, this is satisfied by:

- Carrying at least the **minimum required auto liability insurance** set by the state.

In some jurisdictions, alternatives can include:

- Posting a bond  
- Making a cash deposit with the state  
- Self-insuring under specific criteria

---

## Relationship to Minimum Insurance Requirements

The **state minimum auto insurance requirements** in the ru3.us datasets are a practical expression of financial responsibility laws: they define the **minimum liability limits** that satisfy those laws for most drivers.
