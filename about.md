# About RU3

RU3 is an independent project focused on publishing **structured, neutral reference information** about auto insurance in the United States.

The project exists for a single purpose:

> **To make reliable, machine-readable information about U.S. auto insurance easier to access, understand, and cite.**

## What RU3 Is

- A structured reference for coverage types, rules, and regulations  
- A collection of open datasets with clear documentation  
- A non-commercial resource: no quotes, no ads, no affiliate links

## What RU3 Is Not

- We do **not** sell or broker insurance.  
- We do **not** make recommendations about which insurer to choose.  
- We do **not** provide personalized advice.

For more information, see our [Methodology](/methodology) and [Data Release Notes](/changelog).
