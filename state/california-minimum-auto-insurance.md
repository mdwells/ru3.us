# California Minimum Auto Insurance Requirements (2025)

California law requires all drivers to carry minimum liability insurance. As of **January 1, 2025**, the minimum required limits are:

| Coverage Type | Minimum Amount |
|---------------|----------------|
| Bodily Injury Liability — per person | **$30,000** |
| Bodily Injury Liability — per accident | **$60,000** |
| Property Damage Liability | **$15,000** |

These values are often written as **30/60/15**.

---

## Key Context

- These limits were raised under **Senate Bill 1107**, replacing the prior **15/30/5** minimums.
- The new limits apply to policies issued or renewed on or after **January 1, 2025**.
- Higher limits are common in practice, especially in urban areas with higher repair and medical costs.
