# Auto Policy Cancellation and Non-Renewal Rules by State

**Key notice periods and allowed reasons for cancellation and non-renewal.**

This dataset is provided as:

- CSV: `/datasets/cancellation-nonrenewal-laws.csv`  
- JSON: `/datasets/cancellation-nonrenewal-laws.json`  

## Fields

- **state_code** – Two-letter postal state code.
- **state_name** – Full state name.
- **min_notice_days_cancellation** – Minimum notice days required for mid-term cancellation.
- **min_notice_days_nonrenewal** – Minimum notice days required for non-renewal.
- **allowed_cancellation_reasons_summary** – Short summary of allowed cancellation reasons.
- **source** – Regulatory or statutory citation.

## Usage Notes

- Values are state-level unless otherwise specified.  
- Always consult original state statutes or regulatory guidance for legal decisions.  
- RU3 provides this data on a best-effort, non-warranted basis.
