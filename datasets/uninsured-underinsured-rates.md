# Estimated Uninsured and Underinsured Motorist Rates by State

**Estimated share of drivers who are uninsured or underinsured by state.**

This dataset is provided as:

- CSV: `/datasets/uninsured-underinsured-rates.csv`  
- JSON: `/datasets/uninsured-underinsured-rates.json`  

## Fields

- **state_code** – Two-letter postal state code.
- **state_name** – Full state name.
- **data_year** – Year of the estimate (YYYY).
- **uninsured_rate_pct** – Estimated percentage of uninsured drivers.
- **underinsured_rate_pct** – Estimated percentage of underinsured drivers, if available.
- **source** – Primary data source or study.

## Usage Notes

- Values are state-level unless otherwise specified.  
- Always consult original state statutes or regulatory guidance for legal decisions.  
- RU3 provides this data on a best-effort, non-warranted basis.
